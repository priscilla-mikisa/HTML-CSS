My First Week in AkiraChix

My name is Mikisa Priscilla, a student at AkiraChix,CodeHive program and this is a brief note of how my first week on campus was.

The Good Occurances

  1. We were warmly welcomed by the almuni.
  2. We were given a double meal, one in the evening and the other in the night.
  3. The almuni carried for us our suitcases and helped us settle in really well and fast.
  4. We were given enough time to sleep after the long journey.
  5. We were given an orientation of the entire campus and introduced to the different staff so, communication became really easy.

The Not-So-Good Things I have Experienced

  • The lessons time table was really produced late so, it became difficult to prepare for the next lesson accordingly.
  • The resident building is really big and puzzle-like so, fiding my way back to the rooms became a problem.
  • The daily program was really delayed so, keeping time for meals became really hard.
